Calling all Potterheads: your Christmas decorations just got a whole lot cooler. Why? Because you can now buy this Harry Potter snow village, with locations from Hogwarts, Diagon Alley, The Burrow, and more.

Whether you think Harry Potter and the Sorcerer's Stone is a holiday movie or not (it definitely is), imagine how cool it would be to have the entire Potterverse set up in your living room—or even throughout your home.

The Department 56 Harry Potter Village collection has figurines and buildings from throughout the series. Take the Hogwart's Express and cross the Great Lake with Harry and Ron and make a stop by Hagrid's Hut once you arrive. Hogwarts is far from where your adventures end, though. You can pop into Honeydukes Sweet Shop and then venture through Hogsmeade and visit The Three Broomsticks. But don't forget to stop by Diagon Alley before you head to Hogwarts: you'll need to get your wand from Ollivander's first.

The collection is pretty extensive, so while getting every piece to complete your village right now might not be in the budget, starting with one location and building on over the years is totally possible. Plus, we'll take any reason to look forward to the holiday season even more, including adding on to our very own mini Potterverse.

And did we mention that Department 56 actually makes tons of different themed villages, including Christmas Vacation, A Christmas Story, The Grinch, and more? Yeah, our carts are full.
