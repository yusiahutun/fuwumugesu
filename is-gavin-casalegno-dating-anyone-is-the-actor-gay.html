Is Gavin Casalegno Gay?

No, Gavin Casalegno isn’t gay. He’s just your average TV crush, and it’s obvious everyone would want to keep him all for themselves.

She was his blessing, his rock, his best friend, and his biggest fan. Or, so he said.
