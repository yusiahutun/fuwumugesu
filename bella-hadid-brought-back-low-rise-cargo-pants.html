Bella Hadid Is Bringing Back Low-Rise Cargo Pants

October 05, 2024
blog

Bella Hadid's Y2K streak is still going strong.

Yesterday, the supermodel stepped out in New York City wearing an outfit clearly taking inspiration from the early aughts. Sporting a pair of baggy white cargo pants, whose low-rise silhouette amply showed off her matching high-waisted white underwear, she paired the trousers with a skintight long-sleeved top that featured a yellow-to-white gradient. She rolled up the top for a cropped look that landed just under her bust and finished the look with a pair of ivory boots, black Oakley sunglasses, and a large black tote bag.

Her boyfriend, art gallerist Marc Kalman, accompanied Hadid during her outing. He looked cool in a plain white tank top, baggy gray trousers, and blue sneakers.

Splash News

Hadid has been seen in a variety of different low-rise bottoms throughout the summer.

In late July, she was photographed walking in the Gramercy neighborhood of New York City while wearing distressed low-rise jeans from I.AM.GIA, which she paired with a retro mustard-yellow sleeveless sweater and black heeled boots.

And, earlier that month, Hadid again went the low-rise route when she opted for a unique pair of low-rise Bermuda patchwork shorts with a pinstriped detail. She styled the outfit with a tiny black cropped top, black knee-high boots, and a roomy multicolored shoulder bag.

Gotham//Getty Images

Hadid previously talked to WSJ. Magazine about curating her iconic street style looks.

"I haven't had a stylist in a long time, maybe two years now," she said. "In the last year, it was really important for me to learn that even if people talk about my style or if they like it or if they don't, it doesn't matter, because it's my style."
