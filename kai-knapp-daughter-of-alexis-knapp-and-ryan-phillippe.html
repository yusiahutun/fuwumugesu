Mary Carillo Single for a long time

Mary's been single for a long time now. The NBC Sportscaster isn't in a big relationship since her tragic divorce from her husband Bill Bowden in 1998.

Mary Carillo Previous Marriage with Bill Bowden

“It wasn’t one of those love-at-first-sight deals. We were good friends for a long time. One morning I woke up and looked at him differently, sort of, ‘Hey, he’s kind of cool looking.”

Tennis seems to be the connection that led both of them to one another. 

Mary was also a professional tennis player in the late 1970s and she won numerous domestic Tennis tournament. She won her first pro title in 1977 at the French Open alongside her best friend Joe McEnroe.

Mary's children with Bill Bowden

Mary shares two children with her ex-husband Bill Bowden, named Anthony, 29, and Rachel, 25. She currently resides in Naples, Florida, where she practically raised her children on her own.
