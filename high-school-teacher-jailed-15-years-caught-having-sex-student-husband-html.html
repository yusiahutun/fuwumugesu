A high school teacher who was caught having sex with one of her students by her husband has been jailed for 15 years.

Courtney Speer, 31, was told she would serve seven years of the sentence after pleading guilty to four counts of second degree sexual assault of a student.

The mother of two had sex with the 17-year-old on multiple occasions at her home in Benton, Arkansas, and also in the parking lot of her school.

Behind bars: Former Bryant High School teacher, Courtney Speer, was sentenced to 15 years in prison for a sexual relationship with a 17-year-old student. She will serve seven years of the term in jail

Scene: The mother of two had sex with the 17-year-old on multiple occasions at her home in Benton, Arkansas, and also in the parking lot of Bryant High School (pictured)

Speer’s husband Paul had told police that he had returned home with their two young children to find his wife naked from the waist down standing at the bedroom door.

He said black male, who was known to him, was also in the bedroom naked from the waist down and wearing a condom. 

The incident took place in September 2012 at the height of the couple’s affair. Speer's husband has since filed for divorce.

When police interviewed the student he admitted to having sex with Speer at her home on four occasions.

He also said they had sex in the back of her car in the parking lot of Bryant High School where Speer was an English teacher.

Speer was charged with six counts of sexual assault and resigned her position at the school in October 2012.

Image change: As Speer was led away in handcuffs (left) she looked very different to when she was first charged in 2012 (right)

She pleaded guilty to four charges when making a brief appearance at court in Bryant, Arkansas.

The former teacher was taken to the Arkansas Department of Correction facility in handcuffs.

Eight years of her 15 year sentence was suspended. She could have been jailed for up to 30 years as sex with a student is considered a class A felony in Arkansas.
