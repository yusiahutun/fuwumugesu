Asda is selling Christmas pyjamas for the whole family including the dog

May 07, 2024
blog

Many families enjoy wearing matching pyjamas at Christmas.

But there’s one member of the household that often gets left out – the dog.

Thankfully, your pooch doesn’t have to miss out on the festive fun this year.

Asda is selling a family range of pyjamas – including sweet garments for our furry friends.

For cold winter nights, you can pick up Cosy Crew sets from the supermarket.

The sleepwear is emblazoned with various slogans, including “Bestie”, “The Boss” and “The Real Boss”.
