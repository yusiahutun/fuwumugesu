Shoppers Can Walk 100,000 Steps in These Crocs Sneakers

June 19, 2024
blog

Until a few years ago, I thought it was acceptable to only own one pair of sneakers. I would wear them into the ground, using the same pair for daily walks, workouts, vacations—you name it. However, I’ve changed my tune in recent years, and I now have a fairly large sneaker collection to take me everywhere I need to go (no rips or rundowns in sight!). Since I’m always looking for the next pair to add to my collection, of course I became instantly intrigued by the Crocs Literide 360 Pacer Sneakers.

Yes, Crocs, the controversial shoe brand people love to hate. I’m not ashamed to admit I hopped on the Crocs bandwagon about a year ago; they are, in fact, ridiculously comfortable. It stands to reason that I’d be curious about these sneakers, since they’re actually pretty stylish, especially for Crocs. What really got me, though, is their host of features to keep feet feeling as great as they look. The footbed is designed with the brand’s patented LiteRide foam insoles for support and cushioning. The breathable upper has perforated patterns that mimic a knit fabric, and is super flexible to match your foot’s natural motion. The waterproof shoes have lightweight rubber soles and come in sizes 4 to 11. It’s basically a bonus that they’re on sale for up to 46 percent off on Amazon, bringing them down to $35 in select colors and sizes.

The fun doesn’t stop there. These sneakers are available in nine colors, so you can add some pizzazz to your spring and summer outfits. In addition to neutrals like black, white, and gray, they also come in more unique hues like pastel pink and periwinkle blue.

With all of these innovative features, it’s no wonder these Crocs sneakers are gaining traction, so to speak. They rank in the top 50 of Amazon’s best-selling women’s fashion sneakers, and they’ve racked up more than 3,800 five-star ratings. I love wearing sneakers for multiple activities, so I was especially curious about their versatility—and the reviews did not disappoint. From nurses working long shifts to water aerobics instructors to vacationers, I found glowing reviews detailing their all-day comfort. One such review came from an ICU nurse, who said they’re a “game changer” and likened them to “walking on clouds” even after a 12-hour shift.

I was particularly blown away by a reviewer who said their family walked “over 100,00 steps in a span of five days” during a week-long Disney World trip in the Crocs sneakers—with “no blisters [and] no discomfort” to boot. And if you’re wondering whether they’ll keep you cool? The answer is yes. One reviewer who spent a week in “100-degree hot summer Texas” said they were “airy” and “comfy” for outdoor walks. Another shopper called out their comfort and fit for working out “in 10,000 different environments,” while others raved that they “hold up well in the water” and “can be worn in just about any condition.”

And then, of course, there’s the reviewer who wears these sneakers for everything from running errands to yardwork, who said they’re “an ideal blend of the Crocs comfort and the practicality of a sneaker.” I’m sold.

If you’re as curious as I am about the Crocs Literide 360 Pacer Sneakers, now’s your chance to snap them up while they’re as low as $35 at Amazon.
