Lauren Alaina Shows Off Slimmer Figure at Twilight: Breaking Dawn Premiere

May 19, 2024
blog

Lauren Alaina has been pretty open mouth about her weight loss achievement since dropping 25 pounds, and understandably, she likes to show off her hard work. The Georgia singer hit the red carpet on Nov. 14 to flaunt her hot new look at this week's 'The Twilight Saga: Breaking Dawn' premiere in Los Angeles in a mini dress and heels.

Truthfully, the 'Twilight' premiere didn't need any extra lighting with her there -- Alaina's signature smile was sparkling enough to brighten the path. The 17-year-old blossoming superstar sported a shimmery mini dress for the big movie premiere and seemed completely comfortable showing off her arms in the sleeveless ensemble and her toned legs in a tall pair of heels.

While Alaina wasn't heavy to begin with, she models a noticeably new Lauren Alaina look these days: a happier, healthier glowing version. Sure, she was once a 'normal' high school girl, but now the small town hopeful with a big voice rubs elbows with movie stars, like 'Twilight' famers Robert Pattinson, Kristen Stewart, Kellan Lutz, Taylor Lautner and Nikki Reed (who is married to Alaina's 'American Idol' co-star Paul McDonald), as well as Willow Smith, Jennifer Love Hewitt and Heather Locklear, all of whom also attended the L.A. premiere.

Alaina has dropped weight since her 'Idol' days, toning up her body and aiming for a healthier lifestyle in the process. However, the young country singer told us a few weeks ago that despite reports, she doesn't want to lose any more weight for the sake of her curves. From the looks of it, she's more than comfortable in her new skin.
