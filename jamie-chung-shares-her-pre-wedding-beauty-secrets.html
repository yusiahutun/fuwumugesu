Jamie Chung Shares Her Pre-Wedding Beauty Secrets

June 11, 2024
blog

“I used to be really bad about washing my face, especially after a long night, but I’m pretty strict about my regimen,” she tells InStyle. “I wear my SPF every day and when I wear my makeup, I make sure to really take it off,” she adds. “I use Sens’Eyes by Make Up For Ever ($24; sephora.com). It’s great for taking off waterproof makeup.” The star has also been indulging in face masks more often to lock in moisture and keep dry patches at bay. As for her hands and body, she's stocking up on two new BB formulas by Jergens: BB Hand Cream ($7; ulta.com) and BB Body Perfecting Skin Cream ($13; ulta.com), which defend against sun damage and even the skin tone.

And like most brides-to-be, Chung is just as eager to maintain gorgeous, healthy-looking strands. “In terms of hair, I’ll also use masks but you can even use a home remedy like coconut oil,” she says. Her advice? “Put it on once a week, go four inches from the scalp and make sure that it goes all the way down to the tips,” she tells us. “Just keep it in for 10 minutes and do a regular wash.”

While she’s still mapping out the exact beauty routine she'll choose for her special day, she does know her signature bold lip will be making an appearance. “I think we’ll do a little bit of natural during the day—really just pretty and classic,” she says. “And at night, throw in a bold lip and diffuse the eyes a little bit.” A new hairstyle might even make its debut. “I kind of want to go shorter with the hair,” she hints. Will Chung be the next star to hop on the bob bandwagon? We’ll just have to wait and see. Though, it's safe to say she'll make a beautiful bride either way.
