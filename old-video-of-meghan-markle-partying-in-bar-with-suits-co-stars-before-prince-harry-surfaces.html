Old video of Meghan Markle partying in bar with Suits co-stars before Prince Harry surfaces

blog

Meghan Markle is mostly seen attending formal events since becoming a royal, but an old video of the Duchess during her Suits days has surfaced.

Meghan Markle and Prince Harry have kept their daily life even more private since the royals were reportedly caught in a paparazzi car chase in New York.

The Duchess’ life changed overnight after it was revealed she was dating the prince; she was able to complete her errands without being hounded by photographers and could enjoy a casual night with friends stress-free.

With millions of eyes now fixated on her, those unfamiliar with the former actress are eager to know more about her life before Harry. Luckily, a new video posted by her Suits co-star has surfaced online – partying and hanging with friends just like everyone else.

Old video of Meghan Markle living her best life before Harry surfaces

On September 6, 2023, Meghan’s Suits co-star Patrick J Adams took a trip down memory lane with a short clip of the cast members singing in a bar.

The footage, dated April 1, 2023, shows Meghan sitting next to Rick Hoffman while he passionately sings the lyrics to the remix of Mr Vegas’ Highs High (Kill Em With It). Adams’ wife, Pretty Little Liars actress Troian Bellisario, sticks her tongue out at the camera as the Duchess dances to the beat.

Check out the video on Adams’ Instagram Highlight titled Suits.

Just before posting the clip, Adams changed his Instagram bio to read: “The guy from that show you’re watching on that app because that girl married that prince” – a reference to how Markle married Prince Harry in 2018.

Patrick was among other Suits actors who attended the royals‘ wedding. Meghan starred in the US drama series for seven years as paralegal-turned-lawyer Rachel Zane. She exited after the season 7 finale.

Adams, who played Markle’s on-screen love interest, Mike Ross, appeared at the ceremony with Bellisario.

Other Suits stars present at the wedding were Gabriel Macht, Sarah Rafferty, Rick Hoffman, Gina Torres, and Abigail Spencer.

Meghan celebrates Beyoncé’s birthday without Prince Harry

Meghan and Harry went for a parent’s night out as they let their hair down at Beyoncé‘s Renaissance tour on Friday (September 1). The couple wore their best silver outfits to celebrate Queen Bey’s 42nd birthday, a dress code that the singer personally requested.

The lovebirds hugged and danced together as Knowles performed hits such as Cuff It and Diva.

The fun clearly wasn’t enough for the Duchess as she headed for another night on the town for a second round of Beyoncé on September 4. It was a girl’s night as Harry was absent, while Meghan dazzled in a high-neck tank and a silver skirt to coordinate with Kerry Washington and Kelly Rowland.

Other celebrities spotted in the crowd included Kim Kardashian, Kylie Jenner, Timothée Chalamet, Zendaya, and Tom Holland.
