Lady Gaga shares glimpse of insane marble bathroom which divides fans with a totally unexpected feat

February 11, 2024
blog

Lady Gaga is getting quite candid on her Instagram. Though the singer typically reserves the social media platform for both editorial and promotional pictures of her glammed-up self and her projects, Gaga is showing fans she can be casual too.

The star took a break from promoting her latest film, House of Gucci – and posting all of her fabulous magazine covers as a result of the project – to share a glimpse of her house and how she relaxes on Sundays.

In a mirror selfie posted on Instagram, the Born This Way singer revealed to her 51 million followers her insane marble bathroom, including an unexpected feature in the middle of it. The walls and floors of the stunning bathroom are covered in a multicolor marble, with shades of both silver and gold totally fit for a star.

Posing under a grandiose crystal chandelier, Gaga sported a cheeky off-the-shoulder camel colored shirt and grey short shorts. She put her signature blonde hair up in a messy bun and curtain bangs. The post was captioned: "Happy Sunday" with a sunflower emoji at the end.

Gaga reveals impressive bathroom with mirror selfie

Behind the singer was something that had fans surprised and divided, though it seems like a pretty relaxing idea. The mirror selfie revealed the triple-threat has a couch (or day-bed?) in the middle of her bathroom. 

Plush and made out of a neutral velvety material, the questionable piece of bathroom furniture was topped with pillows and even a cushion in the shape of a sloth's face.
