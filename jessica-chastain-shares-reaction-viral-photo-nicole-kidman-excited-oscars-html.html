Jessica Chastain shared her feelings on the photo of Nicole Kidman looking excited to see her at the Oscars that was misinterpreted as a reaction to the infamous slap.

'So, Jessica, ...what was your reaction to everyone thinking that the viral photo of Nicole Kidman freaking out at the Oscars last year was because of the slap, when she was actually really excited to see you?,' asked virtual fans Lindsay and Sandy.
