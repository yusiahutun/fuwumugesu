Best Valentine's Day Jewellery Gifts 2020 | The Sun UK

May 27, 2024
blog

They say 'diamonds are a girl's best friend', but really, women aren't all that picky.

They're also perfectly happy with pearls and semi-precious stones, silver and gold anything, watches and brooches (including spider ones - we're looking at you, Lady Hale) and pretty much any other Valentine's Day jewellery gift they receive.

After all, nothing does the trick quite like jewellery when it comes to making someone feel super-special on Valentine's Day.

There are lots of different jewellery gifts that will be appreciated on Valentine's Day.

You can stick with the Valentine's theme and choose something hearts and roses inspired, or pink or red, like a heart charm or crystal heart bracelet.

We also love the idea of something personalised, like a customisable Florence London bangle with a special date or featuring your names.

A present with a personal touch can also be a nod to the recipient's personal style. Have a Valentine with an edgy-sophisticated style sense? Then they'll love one of the single statement earrings for unusual piercings from every celeb's favourite jeweller, Maria Tash.

1. Best Valentine's Day jewellery gifts: personalised

A personalised present gives you a unique opportunity to give a jewellery gift that's truly unique and meaningful.

Florence London's 18K gold plated and silver bangles with enamel are handmade to order, and come in a variety of colours and two sizes (petite, 59mm, and regular, 64mm).

Not only can you personalise the outer part of the bangle with names, dates in Roman numerals, initials or a favourite word, you can also inscribe the bangle's interior with an engraved message.

2. Best Valentine's Day jewellery gifts: dangling charm

Thomas Sabo's charms make a lovely Valentine's Day jewellery gift and there are plenty of varieties of heart charm for those who like to stick to the celebratory, romantic theme.

You can find crystal-bedecked ones, or enamel charms in pink or red.

This sterling silver design makes an excellent addition to a bracelet or necklace.
