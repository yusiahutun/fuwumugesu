Outtake photos of Taylor Swift with 'eye bags' at SNL has fans relieved she's not 'perfect'

September 10, 2024
blog

Swifites may feel like they’ve seen every Taylor Swift photo ever taken, but sometimes, in the depths of Reddit, we’re treated to some outtakes we’ve never seen before. This time, they’re from Saturday Night Live.

Taylor Swift is no stranger to the hit TV show, Saturday Night Live, making a handful of appearances, many times as a musical guest. Her latest appearance was a surprise cameo in 2023, where she introduced her friend, Ice Spice. However, her most recent ‘major’ moment on the show was in 2021, performing the 10-minute version of her newly re-released hit All Too Well off of Red (Taylor’s Version). Alongside that came a stunning photoshoot, and over three years later, we’re still receiving stunning outtakes. The most recent has fans breathing a sigh of relief as they realize that even Taylor isn’t immune to bags under her eyes!

‘New’ Taylor Swift photos show ‘she does not stop’

Once again, more outtakes from the shoot have surfaced on the internet. Although fans are over the moon, they’re left questioning “How are there constant new outtakes from this photoshoot??” and how are they all so good?

Of course, they’re not complaining though, as X, formerly known as Twitter went wild in Jan when outtakes dropped. Now, in the newest ‘outtake’ photos, posted on Reddit earlier this week, she has some fans ‘feeling seen’ with the bags under her eyes.

“I knew she had to have bags under her eyes. Girl does not stop,” wrote one fan, as another replied: “I feel seen.”

“I lowkey love these pictures because of the bags under her eyes. How does she look cuter with them?? Doesn’t really matter, still love them,” penned another.

After having ‘not stopped’ for the longest time, embarking on her worldwide Eras Tour, the Bad Blood singer is now enjoying some well-earned time off with her boyfriend, Travis Kelce.

The couple recently jetted off to The Bahamas for a romantic vacation, before enjoying some quality time together in LA.

Taylor will be heading off to Paris in May to kick off her European leg, and Kelce has already revealed he’ll be joining her where he can.

Travis told Taylor he ‘wanted to spend the rest of his life with her’

When the two weren’t making out in The Bahamas, they were ‘deep in conversation,’ and Travis made the sweetest confession, one source claims.

“Travis told Taylor he wanted to spend the rest of his life with her, and she told him she feels the same,” an insider told Life & Style. “It wasn’t an official proposal, but they’re committed to each other, and they spent the weekend celebrating in paradise.”

“It clearly was an intimate moment,” the source revealed. We’ll be waiting for the wedding bells!
