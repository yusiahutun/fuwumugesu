Naked ramblers called “Stark Trekkers” are braving the cold and going on nude rambles.

Despite forecasters predicting plunging temperatures this week, the group are still boldly going into woods and fields.

They have planned a series of walks this month – many several miles long – which end in trips to pubs or cafés.

It adds: “The walks are planned to take place in fairly uninhabited parts of the countryside to minimise the chance of meeting clothed walkers.

“On these walks we usually conclude our day with supper together in the pub.

“The shorter Stark Trekkers walks offer monthly walks throughout the year in Kent and Sussex. They are about half the length of Nat Ram walks. They finish mid-afternoon with a visit to a local tea shop.”

The group said that nude rambling was deemed to be a lawful activity – but added that they cover up when close to a ­property, along public roads or anywhere near children.
