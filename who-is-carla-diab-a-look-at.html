Who Is Carla Diab? A Look at the Reality Star's Life

June 13, 2024
blog

Carla Diab is a Lebanese-American actress, supermodel, businesswoman, philanthropist, and TV personality. She rose to fame after appearing on several reality shows, including “The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills.” However, her TV appearances and influence in the fashion industry have steered her career in the right direction. 

Since she came into the limelight, Diab has made a name for herself through hard work, determination, and passion. As a multi-talented fashion designer and successful businesswoman, she is a force to reckon with in the fashion industry.

Here’s everything we know about the thriving business mogul.

Carla Diab Biography

The reality tv star was born in Rocky River, Ohio, United States, on October 11, 1985. While she hasn’t revealed much about her parents and siblings, online reports suggest she had a happy childhood. According to reports, Carla Diab grew up with her elder sister, Maya Diab, a fashionista and jeweler. 

Although there are no details regarding her elementary education, she attended Rocky River High School. After high school, she enrolled at Cleveland State University and graduated with a Bachelor’s degree in finance. Afterward, she interned at Keybank’s Corporate Treasury Division and completed her internship in 2019. In addition, she received the Awards of Excellence in Finance from Cleveland on April 30, 2020. 

Diab started working as a sales worker at Five Below, an assisted care facility, as a student. However, she transitioned to fashion design in 2001. Carla Diab rose to prominence after appearing on reality TV shows like Project Runway, where she showcased her talent and creativity as a fashion designer. 

Diab impressed the judges and viewers with her designs and soon became a prominent figure. She also gained popularity as the host of the Lebanese edition of “Dancing With the Stars” on MTV Lebanon. Additionally, Carla Diab is a broadcaster at Fi-Male, where she hosts a show every Friday night on LBCI. Aside from these, the reality star is into marketing and promoting the fitness brand Fat2fit.

Is Carla Diab Dating Anyone?

No, she is not. Carla Diab is not dating anyone. She is famous for keeping her personal life away from the media. Also, she has not disclosed any new affair with anyone since her brief stint with her ex-husband ended.   

Carla Diab was married to Tony Abou Jaoudeh, an actor and comedian. However, she kept the news of her marriage private, and the media only got wind of it after their divorce. The famous reality star opened up about her marriage after she and the actor parted ways.  

Carla Diab’s Children

Although Carla Diab is not in a romantic relationship with anyone, she has a daughter. The RHOBH star is a mother to a beautiful daughter, Lea Abou Jaoudeh. There are no details about when and how Diab met her ex-husband, nor when the former couple tied the knot. However, according to her ex-husband’s Instagram, the now-divorced couple welcomed their daughter in November 2009.  

Although Lea is still in her teenage years, the young celebrity kid has a massive presence on social media. However, she has just 12 posts that can only be viewed by her verified followers, as her Instagram account is private. 

Does Carla Diab Own a Yacht?

The actress and businesswoman has posted several videos of herself in a yacht on her social media pages. However, it is unknown if she owns it or not.

Carla Diab Instagram

Although she is known for being private, Carla Diab has a massive social media presence. She has 1.2 million followers on Instagram and over 5k followers on TikTok. She flaunts her superstar lifestyle and showcases her work on social media platforms. Similarly, her daughter has over 14k followers on Instagram due to her mother’s influence. 

What Is Carla Diab’s Net Worth?

According to sources, Carla Diab has a net worth of $5,000,000. Her success in the entertainment industry is from her lengthy career as a model, actress, reality TV star, and designer of eponymous fashion lines. She also earns from promoting and marketing products as a brand ambassador and influencer.

However, her principal source of income is her fashion line and her earnings from TV appearances. Despite her privacy, Carla Diab is renowned for her philanthropy and support of different philanthropic projects. 

Carla Diab has proven that one can be successful with creativity, hard work, and determination. Like Wendy Williams, who faced countless challenges and still achieved success, Diab has become a force to reckon with in Hollywood.
