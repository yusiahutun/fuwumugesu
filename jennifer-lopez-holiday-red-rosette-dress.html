Jennifer Lopez Does Holiday Glamour in Red Rosette Jersey Dress

August 07, 2024
blog

Green may be Jennifer Lopez’s favorite color, but she rocks the other Christmas shade just as well.

The 54-year-old superstar shared a series of mirror selfies to her Instagram account on Tuesday, showing off a mock-neck jersey maxi dress in red from Magda Butrym. The long-sleeved bodycon dress featured a special rosette accessory stitched onto the neckline, which J.Lo’s post gave us a nice view of. She captioned the carousel with a simple red heart emoji.

Lopez’s festive holiday look was styled by the duo Rob Zangardi and Mariel Haenn, while her bouncy waves were courtesy of hairstylist Lorenzo Martin.

The Marry Me actor also tagged her beauty company, JLo Beauty, in the photo, which leads us to believe she got glammed up using her own products, with the help of makeup artist Mary Phillips. Finally, the singer’s sharp, blush-colored nails were done by Tom Bachik, who used Aprés Nail Gel-X Tips and a coat of the same company’s Gel Couleur.

courtesy of Jennifer Lopez via Instagram//Instagram

“Can’t get enough Christmas,” J.Lo wrote over a photo of her holiday fit, which she reposted on her Instagram Story. And we believe it. The Hustlers star recently hosted an extravagant Christmas party with her husband, Ben Affleck, attended by fellow celebrities including Margot Robbie, Michael B. Jordan, Lily Rabe, and Jenifer Lewis. At one point, Lewis and Lopez even broke out into song, which inspired the rest of the partygoers to sing carols late into the night.
