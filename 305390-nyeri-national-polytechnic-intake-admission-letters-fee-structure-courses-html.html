Nyeri National Polytechnic intake, admission letters, fee structure, and courses

blog

The demand for technical skills that help an individual to sustain their economic needs has increased a lot. Most of the people think that the only place they can get the skills is joining institutes located in the cities. Which indeed is not the case. In this case, Nyeri National Polytechnic is a technical institute that offers technical skills from craft, certificate and diploma levels and can deliver what you want.

Nyeri Polytechnic was initially a technical secondary school. But later under the Ministry of Science and Technology, it was made a technical college. At first, it was offering craft and certificate courses. At the moment, apart from the craft and certificate courses, the institution is also offering a variety of diploma courses and short courses in a conducive and well-managed environment.

Nyeri Polytechnic admissions

To get the admission letter for Nyeri National Polytechnic, your qualification depends on the mean grade attained in secondary school for certificate and diploma courses.

Once you qualify for the course, you can continue to get the admission from the college registrar’s office.

Fill in the admission form and attach the required documents then address it to Nyeri Polytechnic with the address provided in the contacts below. The best time to apply is when the Nyeri National Polytechnic intake is running.

Nyeri National Polytechnic fees structure

Fees for all courses are affordable and vary from the type of courses and the period of the course. The amount will be specified once you get the admission letter.
