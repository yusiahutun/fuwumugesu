Biography

Roy Hibbert was born in Queens, NY on December 11, 1986. 7’2″ center who was named to his first NBA All-Star team as a member of the Indiana Pacers in 2012. He spent his first seven seasons in Indiana before a 2015 trade sent him to the Los Angeles Lakers. He is known for his shot-blocking and rebounding. In 2017, he signed with the Denver Nuggets. His Pacers developed a rivalry with LeBron James and the Miami Heat, losing close series to the Heat during both the 2012 and 2013 NBA Playoffs. He was a two-time All-Big East selection during his last two seasons at Georgetown University.
