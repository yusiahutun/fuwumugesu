There’s no time of year better than Halloween to showcase your iconic style. I mean, it’s essentially a normal person’s Met Gala: Everyone is given the chance to channel their inner diva and dress like whatever persona best represents them. It seems like the Halloween costume trends for 2023 are so much more fashion-forward than what we’re used to. I mean, we’ve got the Barbie movie, Black Mirror, and The Little Mermaid to use as inspo.

Typically, popular Halloween costumes are classics—like a witch, a skeleton, or a cat—but instead of keeping to tradition, we’re looking to what’s trending within pop culture. So, in addition to looking to your favorite subscription service, pay close attention to what you see as you scroll through Instagram and TikTok. By taking note of what everyone’s talking about this year, you can easily come up with a fun costume that’s bound to get plenty of compliments. Creativity + Pop Culture Relevancy = Halloween Costume Gold, baby! 

(If you’re worried about how to actually recreate some of these looks, we got you. We’ve included plenty of tips on how to DIY these Halloween costumes wherever possible, so you don’t have to frantically Google your favorite character and brainstorm in the fabric aisle at Hobby Lobby.)

Below, you’ll find a bevy of culturally relevant and incredibly fashion-forward Halloween costume ideas to give you major inspiration. You should have several Halloween outings lined up because you’ll be hard-pressed to choose just one of these Halloween costume trends.

You’re welcome. Now, let’s get spooky!

  • Barbie from Barbie

    There’s no doubt that Barbie (and Ken!) will be the most popular Halloween costume this year. Part of the costume’s popularity will be the seemingly infinite ways people will be able to interpret the costume. You can go as one of Margot Robbie’s Barbie looks, weird Barbie, doctor Barbie, astronaut Barbie, etc.—there’s really an endless trove of options. For inspiration, I’d recommend doing a deep dive into Margot Robbie’s Barbie press tour looks. Perfection.

  • 'Joan Is Awful' from Black Mirror

    If you love a niche costume idea that makes everyone say, “Ohhhh” once they figure it out, then Joan from the Black Mirror episode “Joan is Awful” is a great pick for you. Plus, it’s a great excuse to bleach two pieces of hair or try out a fun wig for an evening.

  • Ariel from The Little Mermaid

    It’s time to reimagine The Little Mermaid costume you rocked as a kid because Mermaidcore has finally had a revival. Take inspiration from Halle Bailey’s portrayal of Ariel in The Little Mermaid and add a shell bra, starfish earrings, and a woven bag to your costume drawer (or maybe just in your everyday wardrobe).

  • Taylor Swift from The Eras Tour

    If you’ve been lucky enough to see The Eras Tour (or if you just watch it every weekend on TikTok), you’ll know that it is one of the most iconic pop culture moments in the making, ever. Taylor Swift has dozens of costume changes throughout the tour which gives plenty to work with when it comes to Halloween costume material. If you’ve already spent hours bedazzling a bodysuit, this is your opportunity to re-wear it.

  • Rihanna from The Super Bowl

    If you’re in need of a clever, last-minute, Halloween costume idea, go as Rihanna from The Super Bowl. All you need is a red jumpsuit and a microphone! If you want to make the costume super clear, carry around a football as an accessory.

  • Queen Charlotte from Queen Charlotte: A Bridgerton Story

    Get real regal with your Halloween costume by dressing up as a character from Queen Charlotte: A Bridgerton Story. If you’ve been looking for an opportunity to wear a full-on ballgown, this is it. Or, you can always do a modern interpretation of the outfit and wear a cute corset with a mini skirt.

  • Wednesday Addams

    Make Halloween a family affair with costumes from The Addams Family. You can build a great Wednesday Addams costume by using pieces from your closet—all you need is a blazer, button-down, tie and pleated skirt. And of course, the two long braids are the ultimate accessory.

  • The US Women's Soccer Team

    Hey, sports fans! Show your dedication to the World Cup by rocking a jersey from your favorite team. This is also a great group costume idea—while you’re at it, take the party outside and play a game.

  • Cassie & Maddy From Euphoria

    If you want to twin with your best friend this Halloween, there’s no better duo than Cassie and Maddy from Euphoria. Throw on a matching set , apply loads of glitter eyeshadow and you’re all set to rule the halls of whatever party you’re headed to next.

  • The Coaches From Ted Lasso

    Dress up as everyone’s favorite fictional coach with this AFC Richmond track jacket , visor and whistle . If you really want to go all out, why not add a mustache? Invite two friends to join in on this costume idea so that you have Coach Beard and Nathan standing right next to you.

  • The Bachelorette

    The Bachelorette uniform consists of sequined gowns on top of sequined gowns. Carry a fake (or real) red rose to complete the look. Who knows, maybe love is in the air and you’ll find someone to give your rose to by the end of the night.

  • Chrissy’s Hawkins High School Cheerleader Uniform From Stranger Things

    Be cute and spooky simultaneously. Grab your pom poms and practice your high kicks to pay homage to Chrissy Cunningham (R.I.P.) in this Hawkins High cheerleader costume .

  • Wanda Maximoff From Wandavision

    If red is your color, then you’ll want to dress up this year as Wanda Maximoff in her cute Scarlet Witch costume. Not only will you look ravishing in red, but also command every room you step foot in this spooky season.

  • Harry Styles From "Love On Tour 2022"

    We’ll keep it quick: Gucci, Gucci and more Gucci. Splurge on the adidas x Gucci Gazelle sneakers that Harry can’t stop wearing. However, if the designer is out of your budget, we found an Etsy storefront that has recreated some of Harry’s signature tour shirts, though you could also just go with whatever sequin numbers you already own.

  • Paris Hilton and Nicole Richie from The Simple Life

    I know that this show debuted like, 20 years ago, but it’s still a winner! Plus, fashion from the early aughts is seriously blowing up on TikTok right now, so this costume still feels fresh. It’s great if you’re in need of a last-minute look because you probably own a bunch of the pieces! Think cowboy boots , denim mini skirts and baby tees.

  • The Cast Of Succession

    Are you a Kendall, Shiv or Roman? Suit up and find out. If you’re going for a more Cousin Greg-inspired look, wear Crocs and socks with your suit. Oh—and carry a water bottle (or two) because of IYKYK. Season three is afoot! Lastly, don’t forget your Waystar Royco merch.
