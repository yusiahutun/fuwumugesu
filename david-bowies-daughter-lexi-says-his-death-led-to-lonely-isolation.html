Rock singer David Bowie’s death in 2016 shocked the world. Now his only daughter, Alexandria is speaking out about the grief she went through.

The Heroes singer died at the age of 69 on January 10, 2016 devastating the music world and fans across the globe. His widow, model Iman, has often spoken about the devastation his death caused her and their family. Now, his 23-year-old daughter Lexi Jones has opened up about her dad’s death and growing up to be “a violent and angry individual.”
