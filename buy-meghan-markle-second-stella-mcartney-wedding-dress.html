You can now buy the Duchess of Sussex's Stella McCartney wedding dress online

July 04, 2024
blog

While the Duchess of Sussex famously chose Givenchy designer Clare Waight Keller to create the wedding gown that she walked down the aisle in, the bride turned to Stella McCartney for the all-important reception dress; her first look as an official member of the royal family.

The elegant halterneck gown drew compliments the world over and doubtless prompted many a copy cat design - but now, for the first time, the actual dress itself is available to buy online.

The dress features a dramatic low back (which we never fully got a glimpse of when Meghan wore it) and full-length skirt which elegantly pools around the bride's feet. It retails for £3,500.

ANA_CUBA

The halterneck style forms part of McCartney's first dedicated bridal collection, which launched on Net-a-Porter this morning (14 November). The range, in true Stella style, is a modern offering for women who want to feel their best, chicest selves on their big day, and features five styles - from a Bianca Jagger-inspired white suit, to a sweeping caped gown.
