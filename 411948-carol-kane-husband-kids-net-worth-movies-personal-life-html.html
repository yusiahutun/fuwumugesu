Early life, parents and Carol Kane siblings

Carol Kane was born June 18, 1952, in Cleveland, Ohio. Her mother, Joy, was a teacher and jazz singer. On the other hand, Carol’s dad, Michael Kane, was a pianist.

Although she has lived all her life in the USA, her grandparents emigrated from Russia, Poland, and Austria. Unfortunately, Carol’s parents divorced when she was only 12. So together with her older sister, Anina, they stayed with their mother.

Education

Carol’s education was hugely affected by her parent’s divorce. Since her mother wanted her to get the best education with minimal disruptions, she took her to Cherry Lawn School. The boarding school in Connecticut was instrumental for the young Carol in different ways.

The main impact of boarding school was introducing her to acting. She started taking theatre classes in two institutions, Professional Children’s School and HB Studios. The two institutions shaped the award-winning actress into what she is today.
