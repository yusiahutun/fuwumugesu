'Good Trouble': Who's in the Cast?

June 23, 2024
blog

If Good Trouble is your guilty pleasure TV show, you’re not alone. Who’s in the cast of Good Trouble? Here’s a look at who appears in the Freeform television series.

Cierra Ramirez

Cierra Ramirez plays Mariana Adams Foster. One of her early roles was in a 2006 episode of CSI: Miami titled “Deviant.” That year, she made her film debut in the movie All In. In 2012, Ramirez joined the cast of The Secret Life of the American Teenager. She played Kathy for 22 episodes from 2012 to 2013. Ramirez is also known for her appearances in The Fosters, Girl in Progress, and The Suite Life of Zack and Cody.

Maia Mitchell

Maia Mitchell plays Callie Adams Foster. One of her early roles was in the television series Mortified. She played Brittany Flune from 2006 to 2007. After that, she played Natasha Hamilton from 2008 to 2009 in the television series Trapped. Mitchell has also appeared in The Lion Guard, The Fosters, and the television series Castaway.

Tommy Martinez

Tommy Martinez plays Gael. One of his early roles was in a 2017 episode of Shameless titled “We Become What We… Frank!” That year, he also appeared in three episodes of Riverdale, in which he played the character Malachi. Martinez is also known for his appearances in The Mosquito Coast and Hunters.

Sherry Cola

Sherry Cola plays Alice Kwan. One of her early roles was in the television series I Love Dick, in which she played Natalie for seven episodes. From 2018 to 2019, Cola played Special Agent Lucy Chun in the series Claws. Cola made her film debut in the 2019 movie Endings, Beginnings. She played the role of Chris. She has also made appearances in Music and Grey Area.

Josh Pence

Josh Pence plays Dennis. One of his early roles was in the 2007 movie Super Sweet 16: The Movie. The following year, he appeared in an episode of CSI: New York titled “DOA for a Day.” In 2012, he had a recurring role in the TV series Revenge. He played Tony Hughes for four episodes. Pence has also appeared in The Man in the High Castle and American Horror Story.

Emma Hunton

Emma Hunton plays Davia. One of her early roles was in a 1999 episode of Judging Amy titled “Witch Hunt.” That year, Hunton also appeared in an episode of Chicken Soup for the Soul titled “Carrie’s Gift.” She made her film debut in the 2005 movie Happy Endings, in which she played the character Becca. Hunton has also appeared in Sonny with a Chance and The Drew Carey Show.

Zuri Adele

Zuri Adele plays Malika. One of her early roles was in a 2010 short production titled Russian Roulette. After that, she appeared in a 2013 episode of the television series Under the Dome. Adele’s other acting roles include appearances in The Social Worker, Plenty, and Spilt Milk.  

Rhea Butcher

Rhea Butcher plays Lindsay Brady. One of their early roles was in the 2016 TV movie Trump vs. Bernie: Debate for America. The following year, they appeared in an episode of CollegeHumor Originals. Butcher then appeared in the series Adam Ruins Everything. They played Rhea for nine episodes from 2016 to 2017. Butcher is also known for appearances in Friendsgiving and Kipo and the Age of Wonderbeasts.
