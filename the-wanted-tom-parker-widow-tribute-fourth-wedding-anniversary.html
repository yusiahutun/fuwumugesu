Kelsey Parker -- the widow of The Wanted's Tom Parker -- is reflecting on her wedding to the British singer on what would have been the couple's fourth wedding anniversary.

Taking to Instagram on Thursday, Kelsey shared a touching tribute to her late husband, who passed away in March following a battle with brain cancer. In her post, the 32-year-old shared a sweet video compilation that featured footage from their nuptials, including several clips of herself and Parker singing together.
