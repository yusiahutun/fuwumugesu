Lancashire women's hockey team strip off for cheeky charity calendar

March 18, 2024
blog

A team of women's hockey players have done away with their kits and posed naked for a charity calendar.

The 14 sportswomen from Lytham St Annes Hockey Club, in Lancashire, bared all for the shots, which show them playing in the buff to raise funds for the Lytham Sports Foundation.

The ladies, who compete in the first and second teams, can be seen practicing their drills wearing only their pants, whilst other pictures show them starkers in the club house.

David Perkins, chairman of the sports club, said: 'I am so proud of all the girls that have taken part in the hockey calendar. 

'Lytham Cricket and Sports Club has always felt like a family and to see the girls supporting our charity to ensure the future of our club continues for generations is brilliant to see.

'The aims of the Lytham Sports Foundation are to raise funds for the ongoing development of sport in Lytham St. Annes and the regeneration of Lytham Cricket and Sports Club, with particular emphasis on the changing facilities and clubhouse.'

The calendars are on sale for £10 and can be bought via PayPal from the Lytham Ladies Hockey Calendar Facebook page.
