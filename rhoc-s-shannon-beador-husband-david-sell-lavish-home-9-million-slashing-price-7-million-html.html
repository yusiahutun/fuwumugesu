Real Housewives of Orange County star Shannon Beador has sold her home after slashing the price by $7 million.

The reality star, 51, and businessman husband David sold their lavish home in Newport Beach's Crystal Cove for $9.05 million, three years after first listing it for $16 million, The Los Angeles Times reports. 

The couple - who went public with David's eight-month affair on the last season of RHOC - recently relisted the seven-bedroom home for $10.9 million.
