University Central Street Art Locations

September 06, 2024
blog

You can obtain the Need For Speed Unbound University Central Street Art Collectibles alongside the 7 Bear Champs and 5 Billboards from the same area only after you finish the game’s prologue. So get over Yaz’s betrayal and start going for the collectibles to earn some extra cash.

In the following guide, we’ll focus on the NFS Unbound University Central Street Art and how to find these collectibles as fast as possible.

NFS Unbound University Central Street Art Locations Map

As usual, we will start with an NFS 2022 map, and this time, we’ve pinpointed the locations of all Street Art in the University Central area or district. Following the same order, let’s see how to find them in the game.

Street Art #1: Next to a Bus Stop

The first Street Art is found on the east side of the district at the corresponding location on our map. After reaching the said location, look for the artwork on the wall next to the bus stop.

Street Art #2: In a Tunnel

The next stop is in the northeastern corner of the area at the second location marked on the map above. If you can’t find the collectible and it seems you are in the right spot, it’s because you’re actually on top of it.

There should be some stairs close enough, by the river. Go down the stairs and enter the tunnel to find the University Central Street Art on a wall. Be careful not to get confused here because there are several artworks in the tunnel; the correct one has a ladder in front of it, just like in the image below.

Street Art #3: On a Small Building by the Elevated Railway

Check our map again and head to the third location marked on it, southwest of Street Art #2. Once in the right spot, look for the artwork on the wall of a small building by the elevated railway.

Street Art #4: Near Some Stairs

Now head west on the street with Graffiti #3 and take the next right. You should immediately find the fourth artwork on a small wall near some stairs on the right side.

Street Art #5: Above a Garage Door

The next collectible is found in the northwestern corner of the area, and you’ll learn the exact location if you check the map above. Upon reaching the place in question, you’ll find the Street Art above a garage door in a parking lot.

Street Art #6: On a Big Building Close to the River

The sixth artwork is also on the west side, like Street Art #5, but a little more to the south, as shown on our map. The Street Art Graffiti is hard to miss due to its size and can be found on the wall of a big building close to the river.

Street Art #7: Above a Door

University Central Street Art Graffiti #7 is found on the south side of the district, more precisely, above the door of a small building at the seventh pinpointed place.

Street Art #8: Next to a Parking by the Elevated Railway

Another Graffiti can be found north of the previous collectible, and you have the exact location marked on our map, of course. This collectible is also hard to miss due to its size, and once you are in the right spot, you’ll see it on the wall of a building next to a parking (by the elevated railway).

Street Art #9: On a Blue Building

Not too far east of Graffiti #8 is the next collectible, so head there with the help of our map if needed and look for a blue building. On a wall of the blue building is the ninth Street Art.

Street Art #10: Under a White Tower

Now head east on the street with artwork #9 and take the next right, then look for the white tower on the left side. The last collectible is on the wall of the building under the tower.
