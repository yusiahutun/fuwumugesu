What is the price of William Ruto's new Lexus?

MSRP is the manufacturer's suggested retail price and is subject to changes at any time because it does not include other fees, including taxes, title license, and dealer fees.

Therefore, the price of a new 2024 Lexus LX 600 varies in different dealerships depending on the trim level.

One dealership estimates the price to be KSh 16,002,000 (US$ 126,000), while others estimate it to be around KSh 30 million.

Engine specifications of the Lexus LX 600

The 2024 Lexus LX 600, built on the chassis shared with Toyota Tundra trucks and Sequoia SUVs, stands out with its lighter frame.

According to Motorally Review, it is powered by a twin-turbocharged 3.5-litre V6 engine. The LX 600 delivers a robust 409 horsepower through a standard 10-speed automatic transmission.

The LX 600 shares components with the Tundra and Sequoia and is similar to the Toyota Land Cruiser.

As a result, the LX 600 boasts a suspension system finely tuned for both on-road performance and off-road adventures.

Lexus LX 600 interiors

The Lexus LX 600 offers a luxurious interior experience. Leather seats are standard across all grades, and an Ultra Luxury trim features a Sunflare brown interior and second-row captain's seats.

Technological features include standard 12.3-inch and 7-inch touchscreens, wireless Apple CarPlay and Android Auto, Wi-Fi hotspot, and advanced safety systems like front collision warning and lane departure warning.

Optional upgrades include a premium audio system and rear entertainment displays.
