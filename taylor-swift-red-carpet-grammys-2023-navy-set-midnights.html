Taylor Swift Dressed Like Midnight Rain for the 2023 Grammys

August 13, 2024
blog

Taylor Swift once again made the whole place shimmer when she arrived on the red carpet at the 2023 Grammy Awards.

The pop star walked into Los Angeles's Crypto.com Arena in a chic navy co-ord by Roberto Cavalli composed of a long-sleeved crop top with a faux turtleneck embellished with dazzling white beads scattered throughout, and a long skirt to match—no doubt an ode to her hit "Midnight Rain" and chart-topping album Midnights.

Getty Images

The pop singer opted for classic glam: a ballerina-esque topknot with blown-out bangs, bronzy makeup, silver eye shadow, a jet-black cat eye ("sharp enough to kill a man"), and her go-to bright red lip. Her manicure was also a metallic shade of midnight blue.

Getty Images

For the night, Swift accessorized with $3 million worth of Lorraine Schwartz jewelry, including 136 carats of natural purple sapphires, paraibas, and kite-shaped diamond earrings. She additionally wore a purple sapphire and white diamond ring, as well as another purple sapphire and a purple and blue sapphire mix ring. (Similar styles are available at ethical diamond brand Brilliant Earth.)

Swift is nominated for Song of the Year at tonight's ceremony for her track "All Too Well"—the 10-minute version.

Getty Images

Last August, she was nominated in five categories at the MTV VMAs and took home three awards for her All Too Well short film, winning in the categories of Video of the Year, Best Direction, and Best Longform Video.

During her speech at the awards show, she also dropped the bombshell that she was releasing a new album, Midnights. She has since dropped the project, which instantly hit the top of the music charts with hits including "Lavender Haze," "Karma," and "Vigilante Shit."
