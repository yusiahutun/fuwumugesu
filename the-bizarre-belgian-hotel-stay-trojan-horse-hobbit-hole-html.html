From cosy Hobbit-like hideaways to a giant wooden Trojan horse, this Belgian hotel certainly delivers on charm.

Known as La Balad des Gnomes, it has 10 unusual rooms that look like they have all come straight out of a fairytale.

Set near the picturesque town of Durbuy, the wooden topsy-turvy rooms feature carved wooden beds that look like they are nestled in the hollow of a tree.

Hobbit hideaway: The bizarre rooms are said to be based on different stories from around the world and turned into a fairytale-style reality

Quirky: From fariytale-style mirrors to toadstools on your dresser, the rooms have some bizarre additions to the decor

Take a dip: Even the bathroom continues with the Hobbit theme, but the bath is probably the size of a swimming pool for Frodos

One room named 'The Legend of Trolls' has a tiny hobbit-sized door, pulley-operated fold-out table and seats nestled into the walls.

Pillars covered in vine leaves are covered in miniature gargoyle faces and the instead of a carpet the floor is covered with a rug of leaves.

And if you fancy squeezing yourself into the room for the night, it will cost you around 150 euros.

Mini retreat: The Hobbit-sized suite has a little table that can be winched into place using a chain and the decor makes it feel like it is nestled into a tree

Cabin fever: The wooden retreat has quirky rooms, from gnome-theme cubby holes to luxurious suites

Owner Dominque Noel's fantasy resort also has another nine rooms with fairy tale themes including the 'A cabin in the forest' and 'Under the moon'.

While from the outside the room named 'The Trojan Horse' looks like a giant wooden pony (Although some might think it resembles a bull), inside guests are welcomed to a huge dining table fit for a king, carved wooden staircase and bath.

Dominque said: 'We decided to make this hotel for people to share our universe and to relive some of our childhood dreams.
