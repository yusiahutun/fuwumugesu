Luther Vandross biography

The remarkable singer suffered from diabetes and hypertension during his adult life. Unfortunately, he experienced a severe stroke in 2003, putting him in a coma for two months.

Luther recovered from his condition, but at a considerable cost. He had lost his ability to speak, walk, and even sing. As his health worsened, Luther reduced his public appearances, the last being on The Oprah Winfrey Show in 2004. He died in 2005 from a heart attack.
