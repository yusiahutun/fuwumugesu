'1923' Fans Are Calling Julia Schlaepfer &quot;An Actual Goddess&quot; for Latest Red Carpet Look

September 01, 2024
blog

1923 star Julia Schlaepfer is known for her stunning red carpet looks. And while her on-screen character, Alexandra, lived a century in the past, the two share an impeccable fashion sense.

In the show, Alexandra jets from Europe to Africa (and spends some harrowing time at sea), and she always looks fabulous doing it, whether she's wearing tailored menswear on safari or intricate flapper-style gowns on an ocean liner. Julia stepped out for a special event in honor of the talented woman responsible for her on-screen looks.

Roy Rochlin

Julia Schlaepfer and costume designer Janie Bryant

The Paley Center for Media recently opened an exhibit dedicated to the Yellowstone spinoff's fashion designs, which were created by Emmy Award-winning costume designer Janie Bryant. Bryant's work can also be seen on 1883, Deadwood, and Mad Men. In addition to the costumes worn by Julia's character, visitors can see clothing worn by Jacob (Harrison Ford) and Cara Dutton (Helen Mirren), and other characters on the show.
