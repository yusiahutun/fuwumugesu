Shop Oprah's Cozy Corduroy Jumpsuit by Rivet Utility

May 24, 2024
blog

Oprah knows a good thing when she sees one — and in this case, it's clear that Rivet Utility jumpsuits have her heart. We spotted the star once again wearing yet another ultra-cozy look by the female-founded brand on Instagram, making this the third time in recent memory that we've seen her rocking a one-piece wonder while at home.

It makes perfect sense why the star would reach for these cool jumpsuits, since they're not only effortless and empowering, but also size-inclusive and made of high-quality, sustainable materials that are ethically manufactured in Los Angeles. It's these differentiators that likely attracted Mandy Moore, Demi Moore, and Gwyneth Paltrow (to name a few) to also suit up in the brand's signature styles in the past, too.

This time around, the media mogul wore the corduroy Big Wig jumpsuit in a pretty periwinkle shade while filming a video throwing out Gayle King's decadent birthday cake in an effort to motivate her followers to get back on track with their health goals and join WW. And while this act of willpower was inspiring, it was (once again) her sleek and soft outfit that stole the show.

Shop now: $395; rivetutility.com

There's nothing quite like a good jumpsuit moment, and we have to hand it to Oprah for reminding us that a softer fabrication can make all the difference when it comes to what we reach for while lounging at home. The Rivet Utility styles that Oprah owns don't come cheap at $395 a pop, so we rounded up several more affordable options that look great for work and play.

This Wild Fang coverall jumpsuit is similarly made of plush corduroy, comes in an eye-catching cornflower blue shade, and nails the utilitarian style that's coming back in a big way. We also spotted a cute boiler suit at Target that's a steal under $40.
