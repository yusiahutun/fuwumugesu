June 14, 2024
blog

Jummah Mubarak is a customary salutation used by Muslims on Jummah. Jummah means Friday, while Mubarak translates to blessed. The phrase can be paraphrased as "Have a blessed Friday". Muslims throughout the world reserve this greeting for use on Friday, which is considered the holiest day of the week. So how do you reply to Jummah Mubarak?

Friday in the Islamic religion is considered a holiday. Most people work half a day and go on leave before the Friday prayer. They take extra care to dress cleanly, bathe, and prepare special meals. It is important to greet other Muslims with greetings like Jummah Mubarak. You can also send your friends and loved ones heartfelt duas, wishes, messages, and blessings.

Can you say Jummah Mubarak to a Muslim woman?

Some scholars view it as a bidah in the Muslim community. A bidah is any innovation that does not have its root in traditional Muslim practice. However, it is allowed for someone to wish a Muslim woman Jummah Mubarak.
