Where to buy Michelle Keegan's outfits from Fool Me Once - but they might surprise you

February 26, 2024
blog

Michelle Keegan's hit thriller Fool Me Once has been a non-stop talking point since it premiered on Netflix on New Year's Day. 

The show, which is based on the novel of the same name by American mystery writer Harlan Coben, has soared to the number one spot on the streaming platform.  

Many viewers have taken to social media to note how 'stunning' the 36-year-old actress is in her role as ex-army captain Maya Stern, who sees her dead husband Joe on a nanny cam. 

Michelle dons a stunning wardrobe in her role as Maya, who has married into the wealthy and mysterious Burkett family and lives in a modern mansion with her daughter Lily. 

The fashion icon wears luxury brands from the likes of Dolce & Gabbana to Burberry - with some surprising items worth thousands of pounds. 

Here, MailOnline reveals where you can get Michelle's exact outfits from the show, as styled by costume designer Jacky Levy. 

White wool coat - Karen Millen £295.20 (previously £369)

Maya's white cool coat is from Karen Millen - but is currently sold out on the website

Fans have gone wild for Maya's white wool coat from Karen Millen - but it is currently sold out everywhere. 

Costume designer Jacky Levy said the timeless piece was bought and fitted around 'a year ago'.  

Maya wears the coat while visiting her late sister Claire's husband Eddie, who has experienced a deep depression and turned to drinking since her death. 

Quilted bomber jacket - Maje Paris £499; Roll neck jumper - Chloe £1,030

Maya wears a Chloe jumper underneath her quilted bomber jacket from Maje Paris

Another statement piece in Maya's wardrobe is her quilted bomber jacket from Maje Paris - and is another item that is currently sold out. 

Maya can be seen wearing it when she drops off her daughter Lily at her mother-in-law Judith's (Joanna Lumley) giant estate. 

She wears it again when discussing plans with her friend from the military, Shane Tessier. 

You'd be forgiven for thinking that the jacket is the most expensive part of the outfit, but the coral-coloured roll neck jumper from Chloe underneath retails at £1,030 alone. 

Shearling biker jacket - All Saints £575 (reduced from £899)

The biker jacket is one of the few items that is - surprisingly - still available to buy

One of Maya's go-to outfits is a biker jacket, which she wears in scenes such as watching her niece Abby playing in her football team.  

Costume designer Jacky Levy has confirmed that the trendy piece is an All Saints jacket, which is currently still available in certain sizes - and has been reduced by over £300. 

However, if you're looking for a cheaper option, you can also get a similar one from Zara for £89.99.  

Miller linen dickey jacket - Veronica Beard £725

Maya's incredibly stylish double breasted blazer is from Veronica Beard

One of Maya's more formal looks comes when she is visiting her husband Joe's fancy private school, the Franklin Biddle Academy. 

Maya dresses the part as she confronts the school's headmaster about what really happened when Andrew Burkett, Joe's brother, died on a yacht many years ago. 

The incredibly stylish navy blazer with gold crest buttons also comes in white and retails for £725.  

You can find a similar one at Sezane for £175 - and it is even called the Michelle Jacket. 

Black tailored coat - Holland Cooper £599 

A newly widowed Maya wears a black tailored coat from Holland Cooper at Joe's funeral

A newly widowed Maya wears a black tailored coat from Holland Cooper at Joe's funeral. 

The scene occurs in numerous flashbacks throughout the series' eight episodes where Maya appears downcast and in mourning.  

Holland Cooper is a brand that the Princess of Wales is also a fan of, so Michelle is in very stylish company. 

Trench coat - Burberry £1,890

A formal tan-coloured trench coat from Burberry costing £1,890 is another designer item in wealthy Maya's wardrobe

A formal tan-coloured trench coat from Burberry costing £1,890 is another designer item in wealthy Maya's wardrobe.  

Maya is also a former army captain so this coat gives a fitting nod to military fashion. 

She wears the coat on a trip to the park where she speaks to her niece Abby and her father Eddie, who had previously banned her from seeing his children.  

The trench has also been worn by the likes of Rosie Huntington Whiteley, Kourtney Kardashian and Sienna Miller. 

But if you're looking for a cheaper option, F&F at Tesco sell one for just a fraction of the price at £45.  

Polo top - Rag & Bone £75

Maya wears a simple black-and-white polo top in some of the show's earliest scenes - including spotting Joe on the nanny cam

In some of the show's earliest scenes, Maya wears a simple black-and-white polo top - presumably to emphasise the fact that she's in mourning. 

She notably has the top on when she spots Joe on the nanny cam - a moment that sets the series in motion as Maya embarks on a mission to find out what has really happened.  

The top is one of the cheapest items she wears in the show - and has now been reduced from £185 to £75 on Rag & Bone. 

Puffer jacket from Perfect Moment £491

Maya wears a stunning and glossy puffer jacket that is perfectly practical for her sleuthing

Maya's stunning and glossy puffer jacket is worn in a couple of scenes with her daughter, Lily. 

The comfortable and practical look is actually a ski jacket - and makes a brilliant wear for the cold Manchester weather and for Maya's demanding hobby of sleuthing. 

The jacket is sold out, but originally retailed for £491 from Perfect Moment.  

Grey tweed coat - Dolce & Gabbana £2,500

Perhaps the most pricey item in Maya's wardrobe is a Dolce & Gabbana coat, which sells for £2,500

Perhaps the most pricey item in Maya's wardrobe is a Dolce & Gabbana coat, which sells for £2,500. 

The herringbone fabric features a cinched in waist and is worn in one of her many heated exchanges with her mother-in-law Judith.
