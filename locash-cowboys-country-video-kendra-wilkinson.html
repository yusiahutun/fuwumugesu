LoCash Cowboys C-O-U-N-T-R-Y Video to Star Kendra Wilkinson

May 29, 2024
blog

This week, the LoCash Cowboys will be shooting a brand new music video for their fan-favorite song, 'C-O-U-N-T-R-Y,' but that's not where the good times end. To kick the already rockin' tune up a notch, they've asked 'Kendra on Top' and 'Girls Next Door' star Kendra Wilkinson to play the lead in the video.

LoCash's Preston Brust and Chris Lucas first let the cat out of the bag during their intimate fan appreciation breakfast last week at the 2012 CMA Music Festival. Then, when Wilkinson arrived in Nashville for a signing of her book Being Kendra: Cribs, Cocktails, and Getting My Sexy Back over the weekend, the country duo officially made the news public.

Wilkinson reached out to the LoCash Cowboys via Twitter, writing, "howdy potnuz ... Can't wait to have some fun with you guys. Woohoo!"

Monday morning (June 11), Wilkinson met with the duo for the first time to discuss the video treatment and game plan for the shoot set to take place about an hour south of Nashville on Tuesday.

"Kendra rocks," Brust tells Taste of Country. "She's just as crazy/sexy/cool as we thought she'd be! She's the perfect girl for our video because not only is she hot, but she's funny and she ain't afraid to get muddy!"

'C-O-U-N-T-R-Y' was originally scheduled to become the duo's fourth official single from their upcoming debut album, but their then-record label (R&J Records) closed its doors before the song had a chance to hit radio airwaves. Not the type to disappoint their fans, Brust and Lucas put their heads together and decided to go forth with making and releasing a music video for the tune anyway.

"It's rowdy and crazy song," adds Brust. "We're real excited about the video coming out and can't wait to see how our fans react."

Stay tuned to Taste of Country for more news about the LoCash Cowboys/Kendra Wilkinson collaboration later in the week.
