Sophie Turner and Joe Jonas Wore Matching Deep V-Necks

blog

Just a few weeks ago, Sophie Turner blessed us all with a photo of her husband, Joe Jonas, with no clothes on to celebrate his birthday. Today, the couple gifted the world in a whole different way, stepping out together in coordinating V-neck collared knitwear, providing some evidence that a couple that matches may be a match made in heaven.

The two kept it casual on the outing, with Turner wearing a green baseball cap and nude-colored face mask with a minty cardigan that she buttoned way down, showing off a deep V that she paired with ripped jeans, a bright blue bag, and White sneakers. And while Jonas didn't wear any green, his striped knit polo had the same V-neck and collar that his wife's top did. He wore the bold top tucked into basic black pants, slid into a pair of Vans sneakers, and he slicked his hair back, which is a marked change from his summer crew cut and more in line with his new foray into midcentury greaser aesthetics.

Earlier today, the two walked with baby Willa in totally different outfits. Turner wore a simple ribbed tank and silky boxer shorts for the occasion, which doesn't just put her in the celebrities-that-wear-more-than-one-outfit-a-day category, but also the celebrities-that-wear-pajamas-as-clothes group. Jonas kept his '50s looks going last night when the two went out for a date, with an open shirt over a tank top of his own and Turner wearing the very Princess Diana combination of a sweatshirt with a tennis skirt.
