How to get Safaricom Bonga points

1 Bonga point is equivalent to how many shillings? You will get one Safaricom Bonga point for each 10 Ksh spent on outgoing calls, SMS messages or internet data. So, it is obvious, that the more calls you make, the more SMS you send, and the more internet you use, the more Safaricom Bonga points you will get.

Did you know that you earn Bonga points when you transact via M-Pesa? Safaricom customers earn Bonga points on the chargeable M-Pesa transactions. These include sending money via M-Pesa, withdrawal of money from an M-Pesa agent or ATM, and payment of bills using the paybill option.

As soon as you earn enough points you can redeem them for different goodies. Redeem tablets, airplane tickets, phones on Safaricom Bonga points. You are able to get your prizes at any Safaricom Retail Center all around the country.

How to activate, check and redeem your Safaricom Bonga points

To become a new Safaricom Bonga Points loyalty scheme participant, you will have to dial *126# and follow the instructions. Once you have completed the registration process, you will receive an SMS with confirmation and as a welcome bonus, you will also receive 10 Safaricom Bonga Points for free.

In case you decide to complete a full registration process and give some personal information, you will be awarded 30 free bonus points. To make this possible, visit your local Safaricom Retail Center or Safaricomâ€™s Customer Care service.

If you want to check the number of points that you have accumulated, simply send an SMS with text "POINTS" to the 124 number, or dial *124#.
