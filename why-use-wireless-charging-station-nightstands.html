6 Reasons Why You Should Use Wireless Charging Station Nightstands

April 28, 2024
blog

Wireless charging has emerged as a transformative innovation, liberating us from the tangles of cords and cables. While numerous wireless charging devices have sprung onto the market, one invention stands out as a game-changer—the wireless charging station nightstand.

This fusion of functionality and style not only caters to our charging needs but also redefines our living spaces. Let's delve into the advantages of incorporating a wireless charging station nightstand into your life and help you decide whether it's worth buying one.

What Is a Wireless Charging Station Nightstand?

A wireless charging station nightstand is simply a bedside nightstand with an integrated wireless charging station that can charge your smartphone (or any other device that supports wireless charging) when it is placed on top of it. It works just like any other wireless charger on the basis of electromagnetic induction.

The best nightstands with wireless charging are functional and elegant and look just like any other bedside table, only with the added benefit of them being a wireless charger for all your devices as well.

Why You Should Invest in a Wireless Charging Nightstand

There are many ways in which a wireless charging nightstand can make your life easier. From small quality of life features to financial and spacial benefits, the following are just some reasons why wireless charging nightstands are an intelligent investment.

1. It Is Convenient

A wireless charging nightstand conveniently sits beside your bed, making it super accessible. Without the need for physical cables, you don’t have to waste time plugging your phone in. It also saves time spent trying to unravel jumbled-up cords, making charging your device even more effortless than it used to be.

It is also a perfect companion for people who tend to forget to charge their devices before going to bed. You wouldn’t have to get up just to plug your charger into the device—simply place it on your wireless bedside table and go to sleep. Additionally, these nightstands often have multiple charging coils so that you can charge several devices at the same time.

2. It Saves Space

Modern living often demands creative solutions to optimize space. The wireless charging station nightstand excels in this aspect by combining two essential elements—the nightstand and the charging station—into one sleek unit. This helps save space in the bedroom.

Wireless charging nightstands also come with drawers and shelves that work as a good storage option for essential items. This thoughtful design also eliminates the need for additional furniture pieces like shelves and cupboards, effectively saving you space and promoting a more organized living environment.

Additionally, wireless charging nightstands eliminate the need for charging cables that can create a mess and take up valuable space on your nightstand. This reduces visual clutter and frees up space.

3. It Is Multi-Purpose

Nightstands with wireless charging are designed to have multiple uses. In addition to charging your devices, there are many things they can be used for:

  • The drawers, shelves, or compartments in the nightstand can store books, magazines, reading glasses, medications, or other items you like to keep close at hand.
  • You can place decorative items such as night lamps and photos on the tabletop.
  • Some models include built-in speakers or alarm clocks for added convenience.
  • Most charging nightstands emit blue, white, or yellow light and, hence, serve the purpose of a really cool night lamp as well.

All these functionalities combined into one bedside table is not just convenient but also saves money. You won’t have to buy separate drawers, chargers, or tables when you can have them all in one.

4. It Adds a Luxurious Touch to Your Bedroom

Not only is it functional, but a wireless charging station nightstand can add a stylish flair to your bedroom decor. They are available in a variety of colors, styles, and premium materials, such as high-gloss glass, tempered glass, MDF, hardwood, and more.

Some models feature integrated lighting elements, such as LED backlight or ambient lighting in different color tones, that not only give your bedroom a contemporary touch but also create a cozy and inviting atmosphere.

5. It Helps You Sleep Better

Wireless charging nightstands can contribute to better sleep indirectly by helping to create a more conducive sleep environment and reducing sleep-disrupting factors. Some wireless charging nightstands emit a warmer nightlight that helps you go to sleep faster. Others, like StoreBound's Sobro Side Table, produce soothing white noise that lulls you to sleep.

Moreover, wireless charging nightstands help mitigate the adverse effects of blue light exposure before bedtime. By keeping devices at a distance, they reduce the disruptive impact of screens on your sleep cycle.

Having a designated spot to charge your devices also encourages you to establish a bedtime routine that includes putting away your electronics. This can help signal to your brain that it's time to wind down and prepare for sleep. You will also be mentally at peace when you go to sleep, knowing that your devices will be charged up and ready to go the moment you wake up.

6. It Is Efficient

Being able to quickly and efficiently charge up multiple devices not only saves time but money, too. A wireless charging station nightstand can help you with just that.

With multiple charging coils, it’s possible to save time as you don’t have to wait for one device to charge before plugging another one in. It also saves money as you don’t have to buy separate chargers for each of your devices.

Additionally, most nightstands have built-in technology that detects when your devices have charged completely and automatically shuts off the charger. This prevents your devices from the risk of overcharging.

Are Wireless Charging Nightstands Bad for Your Health?

While bedside charging is convenient, many people are concerned about the radiation risks of wireless chargers on their phones and their health. The proximity of these charging stations to your head while you sleep might deter people from investing in them. The concerns aren’t entirely unfounded.

Wireless chargers do emit EMF radiation, which, strictly speaking, is considered unsafe for the human body, but they only emit this radiation when in use. And even then, they produce only up to three milligauss (mG), which is really small and nowhere near as dangerous as it seems.

All your appliances emit such levels of radiation. Even standing under the sun has a significantly higher radiation risk than all your appliances combined.

On top of that, the radiation emitted by wireless chargers has a very small range that doesn’t extend beyond the nightstand itself. Therefore, it’s perfectly safe to have a wireless charging nightstand next to your bed while you sleep without worrying about it having any bad effects on your health.

Wireless Charging Station Nightstands Are a Smart Investment

Nightstands with wireless charging stations are a multifunctional addition to your home decor. They are not just functional charging stations that help charge your devices but are also intelligent investments that enhance convenience, organization, aesthetics, and sleep quality.

Some models come with remote-controlled LED lighting as well as USB and Type-C ports for wired charging. You may also want to look for a nightstand with additional storage options, such as drawers or shelves, to keep your bedside essentials organized. Ultimately, the best nightstand for you will depend on your individual needs and preferences, so do your research before making a purchase.
