5 times Cecilia Cheung stunned in luxury fashion looks, from the Hong Kong stars take on Audrey H

August 21, 2024

Cheung first caught the attention of film producers when she appeared in a lemon tea television commercial. She released her first album in 1999 and went on to put out her 10th and final album in 2005.

Her sweet, girl-next-door look quickly landed her leading film roles, too. Her inaugural role in the 1999 film Fly Me to Polaris earned her a best actress award at the Hong Kong Film Awards. She was also nominated for best newcomer at the award for her role in the Stephen Chow-led film King of Comedy the same year.

She later stopped making films and focused her attention on raising her three sons, though was never truly far from the public eye, as fans continued to follow her online and in the news.

In 2021, she appeared in the second season of the Chinese reality competition show Sisters Who Make Waves, proving she still has what it takes to command a stage. Since then, her star continues to rise as her Xiaohongshu followers hone in on her every move, taking style cues from the 43-year-old star.

Here, we go in for a closer look at why her Breakfast at Tiffany’s look captivated so many fans – and decode four other stylish moments.

1. Breakfast at Bulgari

Taking inspiration from Audrey Hepburn’s iconic little black dress look from the film Breakfast at Tiffany’s, Cheung opted to pair her elegant ensemble with a necklace and earrings from Bulgari. She also wore a bracelet from the same collection over one of her black opera gloves.

2. Givenchy in Paris

Wearing her signature long black waves down, she traversed the streets of Paris looking relaxed yet elegant in a knee-length, faux snow leopard coat, a ruffled blouse and a pair of wide-legged jeans from Givenchy. She kept accessories simple with a shoulder bag, a pair of mirrored sunglasses and statement earrings.

3. Golden girl in Valentino

Cheung modelled Valentino’s new StudSign handbag, looking effortlessly elegant in a gold knitted vest. Paired with a pleated miniskirt, she exuded demure youthfulness without being over-the-top.

4. Sporty chic in Celine

Few manage to make a sweatsuit look stylish the way Cheung can. With her pulled back hair, she paired an electric blue Celine hoodie with a pair of matching logo jogger pants. She wore a pair of Louis Vuitton trainers with matching blue accents to complete the look.

5. Balenciaga Barbie

If Barbie ever wore Balenciaga, she might well go for this playful number from the brand’s 2022 spring/summer collection. Cheung wore a long coat with yellow floral graphics in a shade of pink Barbie herself would approve of.

With her hair pulled back and put into an extra-long braid, she kept her make-up to a minimum so as not to clash with the bright pink Balenciaga number.
