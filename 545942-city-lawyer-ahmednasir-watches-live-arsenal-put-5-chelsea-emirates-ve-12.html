City Lawyer Ahmednasir Watches Live as Arsenal Put 5 Past Chelsea at Emirates: &quot;Could've Been 12&quot;

August 31, 2024
blog
  • Ahmednasir Abdullahi, also known as Grand Mullah, was present at the Emirates Stadium to witness the Arsenal vs Chelsea match
  • Arsenal secured a dominant 5-0 victory over Chelsea, extending their lead in the Premier League to 77 points, four points ahead of Manchester City.
  • Ahmednasir expressed his joy on X, jokingly suggesting that while a 12-0 win would have been extraordinary, he was satisfied with the 5-0 result

City Lawyer Ahmednasir Abdullahi, alias Grand Mullah, is an avid supporter of Arsenal FC.

Ahmednasir joined a sea of fans at London’s Emirates Stadium on Tuesday evening, April 23.

Ahmednasir overjoyed after Arsenal's victory

They gathered to witness the clash between Arsenal and Chelsea in a much-anticipated football match.

The lawyer was seen celebrating as Arsenal delivered a stunning performance, defeating Chelsea with a resounding 5-0 victory in the London derby.

This triumph propelled the Gunners to a four-point lead over Manchester City, the three-time consecutive Premier League champions, who have played two games less and have six more to go, standing at 77 points.

Who scored Arsenal's goals against Chelsea?

Despite Chelsea showing potential with several opportunities in the first half, the situation looked bleak after Mauricio Pochettino’s team conceded the first goal by Leandro Trossard in the 4th minute.

The match’s momentum shifted irreversibly after Arsenal’s second goal.

Ben White doubled Arsenal’s lead in the 52nd minute, and Kai Havertz scored twice against his previous team, bringing the score to 4-0 by the 65th minute.

White then inadvertently netted the ‘Goal of the Season’ at the 70th minute, escalating the score from 1-0 to 5-0 within just 18 minutes.

Expressing his elation on X, Ahmednasir shared an image of himself donned in a long coat, sporting a cheerful grin.

His caption read:

"It could have been 12.0. But 5.0 is good result."

Arsenal fan tears jersey after defeat by Aston Villa

On Sunday, April 14, a distraught Arsenal fan tore his jersey and wailed in anguish following the team's defeat against Aston Villa.

The video of the disappointed Arsenal fan's outburst went viral.

In a video shared online by user Kilundeezy, the fan tore his Arsenal jersey and shouted, drawing cheers from fellow club-goers.
