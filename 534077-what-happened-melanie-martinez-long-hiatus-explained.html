Who is Melanie Martinez?

Melanie Martinez is the daughter of a Dominican mother, Mery, and a Puerto Rican father, Jose Martinez. She was born on 28 April 1995 in Astoria, New York, United States. She is 28 years old as of January 2024. Her zodiac sign is Taurus.

The alternative pop music singer was raised alongside her younger brother, Joseph Timothy Martinez. She is an American national of Latina ethnicity. She resides in New York City, New York, United States.

Melanie attended Plaza Elementary School, where she started honing her songwriting skills. Later, she took her high school education at Baldwin High School.
