Bella Hadid reclaims her crown as the queen of street style in two vintage-inspired looks

August 23, 2024
blog

It feels like forever since Bella Hadid has graced the sidewalks of New York City with one of her winning street-style looks. But — praise the heavens — the drought seems to officially be over. As the supermodel prepares to launch her new fragrance line, Orebella, she showed out today to deliver two awe-inspiring looks — each packed with a vintage twist.

First on the agenda was a romantic nude slip dress with a corseted bodice, fluttery butterfly embellishments adorning the torso, a draped waist, mesh overlay, and a swingy fringe hemline. Fans of Hadid’s wardrobe are likely well acquainted with the model’s love of vintage treasure hunting, and this outfit demonstrates that habit perfectly, with the dress hailing from John Galliano’s Dior spring/summer 2004 collection. She completed the outfit simply, opting for matching pointed-toe mules and a delicate chain choker necklace.

Later in the day, Hadid changed into several all-black pieces from Ferragamo’s spring/summer 2024 runway, putting on an outfit reminiscent of a look we might expect Rizzo from Grease to wear. This 1950s revival came in the form of high-rise capri pants, sensibly paired with a cropped nylon zip-up bomber jacket and sleek leather mules with a slim curved heel. For a modern edge, she accessorised with silver-framed sunglasses and an array of chunky gold rings.

Getty Images

Orebella will officially launch with three scents in the US today. Hadid recently opened up about the passion project in an Instagram post. “Can’t believe I’m almost able to share what I’ve been keeping from you… 5+ years of creativity, tears, magic, and refuelling of my life,” she wrote. “My true passion. For you and me and everyone in between! I’m so excited for you all to be a part of @orebella!”
