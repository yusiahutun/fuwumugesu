I Finally Found a Dainty Gold Chain to Complete My Layered Necklaces

July 28, 2024

I've been fascinated with jewelry for as long as I can remember. On family vacations as a child, my siblings would pick out souvenirs like stuffed animals and T-shirts, but I would spend all my time at the jewelry stand deciding on which ring, bracelet, or necklace I wanted. My obsession has only grown with time, but my taste has obviously evolved. As of late, my favorite jewelry formula — whether I’m wearing an oversized crew neck and jeans or a dress with heels — is a pair of vintage statement stud earrings and two to three gold layered necklaces. 

A few months back, I couldn’t put my finger on what kept looking “off” every time I went to choose which necklaces to pair together. I was trying on different lengths and various style chains but simply wasn’t satisfied with the results. However, the problem was solved when I added this gold dainty chain from GLDN to my collection.

At its shortest, it’s the ideal first layer and hits right at my collarbone, while at its longest, it’s become the go-to chain for one of my many pendants.
