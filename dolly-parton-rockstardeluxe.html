Dolly Parton Celebrates Birthday With Release of ROCKSTAR DELUXE

February 28, 2024
blog

Dolly Parton ROCKSTAR (Courtesy of Butterfly Records - Photo: Vijat Mohindra)

NASHVILLE (CelebrityAccess) – Beloved global icon Dolly Parton is celebrating her birthday this year by surprising fans with a deluxe version of her genre-bending ROCKSTAR album – ROCKSTAR DELUXE is available now on all digital streaming platforms.

Parton’s first venture into the Rock genre proved to be a success, marking her highest-performing album in sales during its debut week. The album achieved an impressive feat by securing six No. 1 positions on the Billboard charts. Notably, it secured the fifth spot on Billboard’s list of top-selling Country albums for the year 2023.

The extensive 39-song collection showcases a stellar lineup of some of the music industry’s most prominent artists. They join forces to reinterpret 21 classic Rock songs, seamlessly blending them with 13 original tracks penned by Parton herself. This ambitious project stands as a testament to Parton’s versatility and impact across music genres.

Now, in honor of her birthday, the legendary entertainer is giving fans even more Rock with the release of ROCKSTAR DELUXE, which includes 9 additional tracks – five previously exclusive retailer tracks and four that have never been released.

Additional tracks for ROCKSTAR DELUXE:

31. Rockin’ It (Live)
32. Hit Me With Your Best Shot
33. Mama Never Said
34. Two Tickets To Paradise
35. Jolene (featuring Måneskin)
36. Stay Out Of My Bedroom
37. Bridge Over Troubled Water
38. The Entertainer
39. Sweet Dreams (Are Made Of This)
