A Celebration of Life

Coping® magazine is proud to be an ongoing national sponsor of National Cancer Survivors Day® (NCSD). Celebrated on the first Sunday in June, NCSD is the day set aside each year to recognize cancer survivors, raise awareness of the ongoing challenges cancer survivors face, and celebrate life.

This 35th annual Celebration of Life was held on Sunday, June 5. In cooperation with the National Cancer Survivors Day Foundation, Coping is pleased to provide you with exclusive coverage of NCSD 2022 (PDF). Due to space limitations, photo submissions most representative of NCSD have been chosen.

National Cancer Survivors Day® 2023 will be held on June 4, and now is the time to get involved! Visit ncsd.org or email info@ncsd.org for more information.
