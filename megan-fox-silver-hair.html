Megan Fox Has Silver Hair Now

August 03, 2024
blog

Megan Fox is going all out for her latest role.

In a new selfie posted to Instagram, Fox looked like a literal silver fox in her new sleek silver hair color, ostensibly for her role in Johnny & Clyde.

"This is what the devil's daughter looks like," she captioned the post. "#JohnnyAndClyde Coming Spring 2022."

Fox tagged makeup artist Clarissa Luna in her post, along with hair stylist Miles Jeffries, who also reposted the photo, writing, "Major Hair transformation for @meganfox."

The film, according to Deadline, is a new take on Bonnie and Clyde, and also stars Tyson Ritter. It follows Fox as crime boss Alana and Ritter as her head of security, who run a prosperous casino.

After somewhat of a hiatus from Hollywood, Fox is returning to the screen in a big way. Earlier this week, she shared photos of herself in costume on set for The Expendables 4, teasing what looks to be an action-filled ride for her character.

In addition to Expendables and Johnny and Clyde, Fox has five other projects lined up, including Big Gold Brick, an upcoming movie in which she stars alongside Oscar Isaac and Andy Garcia. The season of Megan Fox, it seems, is only just getting started.
