Paul Marsden Barton is a former foreign exchange broker. 

He is recognized as the husband of former Irish model Nuala Quinn-Barton and father of actress Mischa Barton. 

Married 

He got married to Nuala Quinn-Barton in 1975.  He is spending blissful life with his beautiful wife to date. 

They have three children together.

Nuala Quinn-Barton is an Independent film producer, former model, and talent manager. 

She came to prominence for producing films such as ‘Homecoming,’ ‘The Third Half,’ ‘Lawn Dogs,’ and many more.

She was born in Newry, County Down, Ireland. She moved to London for her studies. She began her career in modeling at a very young age. 

His Daughter, Mischa Barton

Mischa Barton is a popular British-Irish- American actress and model. 

She is best known for her role as Marissa Cooper in the teen drama television series ‘The O.C.’ She bagged two Teen Choice Awards for her performance in that series.

She was born on 24 January 1986, Hammersmith, London, England. 

She was raised with her two sisters. Her family moved to the United States when she was only five due to her father’s work. 

She began her career in acting when she was only eight years old. She made her film debut from drama movie ‘Lawns Dogs.’ 

She is not married yet.
