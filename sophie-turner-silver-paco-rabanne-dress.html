Sophie Turner Wears Sheer Silver Paco Rabanne Dress to Chasing Happiness Premiere with Joe Jonas

blog
  • Sophie Turner stunned in a silver Paco Rabanne dress at the Chasing Happiness premiere with Joe Jonas last night.
  • The actress makes a brief cameo in the documentary in personal clips with husband Joe.

Sophie Turner is killing it on the red carpet once again, this time in Paco Rabanne. Last night, the Game of Thrones and X-Men star accompanied her husband, Joe Jonas, to the premiere of Chasing Happiness, the new documentary that follows the Jonas Brothers's journey.

After promoting GoT and Dark Phoenix back-to-back, walking the carpet in L.A. last night was basically just another day for Turner, but her look was anything but boring. She wore a silver chainmail dress from Paco Rabanne's Fall 2019 collection, which featured disc sequins and a nude slip underneath for a nearly sheer effect.

Axelle/Bauer-Griffin//Getty Images

Joe Jonas and Sophie Turner at the ’Chasing Happiness’ premiere.

Peter White//Getty Images

Turner’s dress appears layered under another look when it hits the runway during Paco Rabanne’s Fall 2019 fashion show.

She kept her hair down in loose waves and wore deep red lipstick to contrast with her high-wattage dress. For footwear, she wore simple black sandals.

Turner looked like such a star that her proud husband, dressed sharply in a plaid grey suit, had to snap a few photos right then and there.

Axelle/Bauer-Griffin//Getty Images

Joe Jonas snaps photos of Sophie Turner on the carpet.

Although the film focuses on Joe and his two brothers, Sophie also makes a brief appearance at the end when her hubby discusses how finding himself led to finding a partner. The scene includes sweet personal footage of Turner with Jonas, from boat escapades to lazy moments in bed. "The impact of falling in love has made me want to be a better man, a better person, and ultimately, it made me a better brother," Joe says of his wife.
