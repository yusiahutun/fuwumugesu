How did Chris Perez and Vanessa Villanueva Meet?

Nonetheless, Vanessa and Chris began dating soon after they met, and after dating for three years, they decided to tie the knot. Hence, Chris tied the knot with his second wife in October 20, 2001. Only close friends and family attended their private wedding ceremony.

Divorce.
