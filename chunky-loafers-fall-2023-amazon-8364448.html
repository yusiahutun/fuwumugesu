This Chunky Shoe Is a Celebrity Staple, and I Found 6 Amazon Pairs

September 11, 2024
blog

Each fall I get so excited to add a pair of knee-high boots to my wardrobe only to remember I don’t really actually wear them that often. For me, the autumn-staple is pretty situational: Am I wearing a mini skirt? Is it under 60 degrees? So this year, rather than grabbing yet another pair of riding boots, I decided to lean into the practical shoe trend celebrities seemingly can’t get enough of. 

Chunky loafers are quickly becoming fall’s ‘It’ shoe, worn by stars across every generation including Hailey Bieber, Emma Chamberlain, Scarlett Johansson, Jennifer Garner, and Jane Fonda. The oversized style brings a cool-girl edge to the otherwise-sophisticated staple and looks good with everything from jeans and a basic tee to a sexy slip dress and jacket (and, yes, even tube socks). And I just found six styles you can grab on Amazon, with prices starting at just $39.

Dream Pairs Chunky Loafer

Dream Pairs’ $39-style is shockingly chic, with a chunky, gold chain detail shoppers say gives the shoes a “very modern” look. This style is available in four colors — black, brown, burgundy, and white — and features a lug-sole bottom, a look we’ve seen Selena Gomez rock. As for the feel, customers say these are “extremely comfortable.” “The chunky sole adds a fashionable touch while providing excellent support and cushioning for my feet,” that same person said, while another fan added that they’re also “easy to walk in.” 

Sam Edelman Laurs Loafer

If you prefer a more timeless look, consider Sam Edelman’s Laurs Loafer, which expertly balances the chunky style with a sleek design. This leather style features a chunky platform and a slim gold band at the top of the shoe. According to shoppers, these are “very comfortable out of the box” and “super cute.” Another person who called them “super comfy” deemed the style a “must have,” explaining that the pair “looks great with jeans and a casual shirt.” And per a customer who wore the style for more than a year, they still “look brand new and have kept [their] shape,” calling them “comfortable and sturdy.”

Franco Sarto Carolynn Lug-Sole Loafer

Franco Sarto’s Carolynn Loafers are my personal favorite thanks to their classy tassel detail and shiny leather exterior (and, yes, it helps that they’re also 40 percent off). This style is a shopper favorite, with more than 3,900 five-star ratings that rave about their “superb quality.” “These are super cute, fit like a glove…and feel like you're walking on a cloud,” said an “obsessed” customer. Another person wrote that they get “tons of compliments” on the “supportive,” “lightweight” loafers while an additional customer called them a “great fall wardrobe addition.”
