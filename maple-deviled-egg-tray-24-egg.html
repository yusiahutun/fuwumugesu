Impress your guests with this Maple Deviled Egg Tray, perfect for any party. Designed to hold 24 eggs, ideal for gourmet deviled egg tray and other appetizers. The tray can also be used as a serving plate by simply turning it upside down. Great multifunctional piece for any kitchen.

Each piece is made from American hardwood and will vary in grain pattern and color. These features make each piece unique, and we think that makes them even more special.
