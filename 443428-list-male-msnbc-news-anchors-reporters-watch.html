1. Jose Diaz-Balart

Top on MSNBC anchors list is Jose Diaz-Ballart. He was born in November 1960 in Fort Lauderdale, Florida. Jose debuted his career working in print journalism during the 1980s. Jose is also renowned for his role as the former anchor of Noticias Telemundo's weeknight editions. He is the recipient of several awards, including four national Emmys, for his outstanding work as a news anchor. Jose is happily married to Brenda, and they have two daughters, Katrina and Sabrina.
