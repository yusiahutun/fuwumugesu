Bye, raccoon eyes: 7 tips for sweat-proof make-up this summer, from beauty influencer Huda Kattan

August 26, 2024
blog

Don’t you hate it when your make-up melts off as soon as you step outside into the heat, leaving you feeling like you’re wearing watercolour paint?

Whether you’re headed to a sizzling summer party, a day at the beach or an outdoor adventure, combating the challenges posed by heat, humidity and sweat is essential for keeping your make-up intact all day long.

Wondering how to achieve a sweat-proof make-up look that will stay put no matter how hot it gets? Here are some strategies, products and application techniques you can rely on.

1. Keep your skincare simple

The foundation for a long-lasting, sweat-proof make-up look starts with properly prepping your skin. “Start by cleansing your face with a gentle, oil-free cleanser to remove any excess oil and impurities,” says Los Angeles-based celebrity make-up artist Carola Pizarro. “Follow up with a lightweight, oil-free moisturiser that won’t weigh your skin down or clog your pores.”

2. Application techniques matter

The way you apply your make-up can make a significant difference in its longevity during hot summer days. For example, instead of applying a thick layer of foundation all at once, building it up in thin layers will not only provide better coverage but also increase the foundation’s staying power.

Another hack is “baking” your make-up after applying concealer by dusting translucent powder under your eyes or any spots where your skin tends to crease, and letting it sit for five to 10 minutes. After time has passed, dust off the remaining product to reveal how the heat from your skin helped set the make-up, ensuring it stays put.

3. All hail primer

Primer is a superhero all year long, but even more when it’s hot. This godsend acts as a barrier between your skin and make-up, creating a smooth, pore-refined canvas for better product adhesion.

By forming a protective shield, primer prevents excess oil and sweat from breaking down your make-up throughout the day, improving its longevity and helping to control shine and blur imperfections, ensuring your make-up stays put.

4. Waterproof products are your best friend

Opting for long-lasting formulas is a must. Look for water-resistant or waterproof products formulated to hold up against sweat and humidity. “For your eyes, waterproof mascaras, eyeshadow sticks and eyeliners are great choices to avoid ‘raccoon eyes’, especially if you plan to hit the pool or beach,” Pizarro advises.

5. Build your foundation

Regarding foundation, go for oil-free and long-wear formulas with matt or semi-matt finish, as they offer buildable coverage while battling shine and maintaining a fresh look throughout the day. Mineral-based foundations are also worth considering, as they tend to be more resistant to heat and sweat. As for blush and bronzer, cream-based ones tend to have better staying power than powders.

6. Setting spray

A setting spray is a game-changer for locking your make-up in place. Not only does it work like a protective shield, but it also ensures your make-up stays flawless from morning to night, no matter what the weather throws your way.

After you’ve applied your make-up, hold the spray about a foot away from your face and lightly mist it. Then, let it air dry completely and avoid touching your face after application to prevent any smudging or smearing.

7. Touch up on the go

Carrying a few touch-up essentials with you can help your make-up stay fresh and sweat-proof throughout the day. When oiliness and sweatiness strike, you’ll be prepared with blotting papers to remove sweat and excess oils without disrupting your make-up. If your complexion needs a radiant boost, lightly dust on a finishing powder with a touch of luminosity to bring back that dewy glow, or go for a pressed powder compact for quick touch-ups without the mess. A face mist can also be a game-changer, delivering moisture while refreshing your make-up without mishaps.
