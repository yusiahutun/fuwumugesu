Who are Henry Cavill’s parents?

Henry was born to Marianne and Colin Cavill on May 5, 1983. Per People, the Argylle actor was raised in Jersey, a small British island, and he often credits his mum and dad for his success and ability to stay modest.

“My upbringing has kept me grounded. My amazing parents have always told me to remain humble and thankful for what I get,” he told the Daily Record in 2013.

He added that they are “very practical people”. “I remember going, ‘I’m really excited about this – I really want it to happen. Who are Henry Cavill’s four brothers?

1. Piers

As reported by People, Piers is the oldest of the Cavill brothers. He reportedly attended boarding school and was an officer in the British armed forces. According to his LinkedIn profile, he currently works as a director at Jersey Laser Scanning in St Helier, Jersey.

2. Niki

Henry’s second oldest bro gained recognition for his work as a soldier for the Royal Marines.

According to the BBC, he made an MBE by Queen Elizabeth for his service as “a courageous field soldier” in Afghanistan.

3. Simon

Not much is known about Henry’s ultra-private older brother Simon, but the actor did once talk about him once in an interview on Jimmy Kimmel Live! “My [older] brother, one up, was four years older than me.
