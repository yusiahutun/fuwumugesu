Mark Wahlberg wanted to show how much he really loves his wife.

The actor shared a few tender, hands-on moments with the mother of his children, Rhea Durham, on Saturday, and on the playing field of all places.

The happy couple were actually there to watch their six-year-old son, Michael, play with his soccer team, but managed to sneak in some very public displays of affection as well.

Make-out session: Mark Wahlberg and his wife, Rhea Durham, got in plenty of PDA during their son's soccer game on Saturday

Mark and Rhea were standing on the sidelines at the park located in the Brentwood neighbourhood of Los Angeles when the actor made a play for gorgeous Rhea.

He was seen wrapping his arms around her and kissing her most romantically while their nine-year-old daughter, Ella Rae, stood nearby.

Mark, 41, must have had quite the effect on dark-haired Rhea, 34, for she appeared to be pulling him in closer.

'Missing you:' Mark has been filming Lone Survivor in New Mexico and definitely felt the need to show Rhea how much he appreciates her

Soccer dad: Mark came to show support for his son, Michael, six, who was playing with his team The Panthers

Family man: Mark and Rhea also brought their daughter, Ella Rae, nine, and son, Brendan, four, along to the game, but left two-year-old Grace at home with the nanny

The Planet of the Apes star was dressed casually in beige T-shirt, khaki shorts and white trainers, while Rhea looked smart in skinny grey jeans, a pink and grey striped shirt and flat-heeled ankle boots.

The Oscar nominee spent much of October on a remote ski mountain in New Mexico filming the action flick Lone Survivor, so perhaps he was just glad to be home again with his spouse and their children.

Mark looked like he was having a fun time with the children as well, including four-year-old Brendan who came to watch his elder brother kick it out with his team.

Footballer: Mark shows his stuff on the field playing a game of toss with his children

Fun in the sun: Mark and Rhea both look happy and smiling after their kiss-filled day

Mark and Rhea have another daughter, two-year-old Grace, but it seems they left her at home with the nanny while they attended the soccer game.

When he wasn't making out with Rhea, the actor-turned-producer took time out to toss a football with the youngsters, throwing and catching it expertly and proving that he is a sportsman at heart.

Mark showed that he's just a regular guy like the other fathers by posing with them and the soccer team for a group picture while Rhea looked on proudly.

Mark and Rhea, a fashion model, became a couple in 2001 but didn't get married until after they'd had three children - in a private Catholic wedding in August 2009. Their daughter, Grace, was born the following year in 2010.
