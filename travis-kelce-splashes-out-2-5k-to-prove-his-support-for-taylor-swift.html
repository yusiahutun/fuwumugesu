Travis Kelce splashes out '$2.5k to prove his support for Taylor Swift'

September 27, 2024
blog

Taylor Swift’s new concert movie just had its premiere, and ‘supportive’ Travis Kelce’s one gesture is proof he’s a keeper.

The Pop Music wizard Taylor Swift is not yet done with her record-breaking world takeover The Eras Tour. But for the fans who couldn’t get tickets to the show, the Daylight crooner is releasing a concert movie showcasing the concert. Now, amidst the premier and re-release of the 1989 album, one certain Kansas City Chiefs player is paying extra attention to the Grammy-winning celebrity.

Travis Kelce splashes out ‘$2.5k for romantic gesture’

Travis Kelce is proving he is not only an NFL heartthrob but also a supportive beau. His kind gesture for Taylor Swift is winning fans’ hearts.

The 34-year-old sports star reportedly sent the Cruel Summer crooner a floral arrangement worth $2,500 to celebrate the Eras Tour premiere.

While Travis couldn’t attend the red carpet in person, his sweet gesture proves he is cheering for the Lover crooner.

“Taylor is having the time of her life”

At 33, Tay Tay is setting new standards for the music industry. Insiders claim the singer is also having the time of her life in this new phase. It’s clear something has changed as fans see her attending Kansas City Chiefs matches.

A source revealed: “Taylor is having the time of her life at these football games, cheering on Travis.

They added: “Her and her friends are fully embracing this era and are having a lot of fun being there.”

Taylor has put on tremendous effort for her elaborate Eras Tour performances, and the source says that she is enjoying herself now.

“Taylor has worked very hard this last year, and it’s fun for her to bring all of her friends along, let loose, and show support.”
